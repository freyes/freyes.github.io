Emacs Tips

Tue 01 January 2013
By Felipe Reyes
  • If you start emacs with "emacs -q" and the problem goes away, the problem is on your end: check your .emacs.
  • If "emacs -q" has problems and "emacs -Q" does not, it's caused by your distro or local admin.
